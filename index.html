How to Buy KishuFather

01

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (browser extension wallet) or TrustWallet (mobile app wallet).

02

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask/Trust Wallet

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance).
Withdraw the BNB tokens to your ERC20 wallet address.
The address starts with a "0x".

03

Go to Pancakeswap and swap for KishuFather Inu

Go to Pancakeswap and connect your wallet.
Paste our contract address to select KishuFather.
Set the slippage tolerance to 1-2% (sometimes it may be bigger depending on the market demand) and swap BNB for KishuFather.
